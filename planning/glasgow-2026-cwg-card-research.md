## Editorial decisions

- The Games run from 23 July to 2 August 2026 in Glasgow.
- Official Glasgow session times were converted from British Summer Time to Sydney AEST by adding nine hours. Cards crossing midnight use the following Sydney calendar day.
- The feed uses 32 selective session cards rather than duplicating every individual medal event. The set covers Australian group fixtures and medal/final sessions across the complete Glasgow programme.
- Rugby Sevens, Cricket and Hockey remain fixed selector filters but have no Glasgow 2026 cards because they are not in the official 2026 programme. No placeholder events were invented.
- Actual programme sports outside the selector's first nine named filters—3x3 Basketball, Bowls, Judo, Weightlifting and Para Powerlifting—map to the locked slot-10 `Miscellaneous` filter.
- All records are `upcoming`, use preview-only storyline copy, have an official schedule URL, clear the feed relevance floor, and use the confirmed Australian broadcast label `Seven / 7plus`.

## Card coverage

- 3x3 Basketball: Australian opening session and medal finals.
- Artistic Gymnastics: team, all-around and apparatus finals.
- Athletics and Para Athletics: headline 100m finals plus selected medal nights and closing relays.
- Bowls and Para Bowls: closing medal session.
- Boxing: both finals sessions.
- Judo: lightweight, middleweight and heavyweight medal sessions.
- Netball: Australia v England, Malawi and South Africa, both semifinals, and the gold-medal match.
- Para Powerlifting: all four finals across the two official sessions.
- Swimming and Para Swimming: opening finals/4x100m relays and closing finals/medley relays.
- Track Cycling and Para Track Cycling: all four evening finals sessions.
- Weightlifting: opening and closing finals.
