## Implementation guardrails from this audit

- `status: completed` requires a completed event plus a recap treatment; `upcoming` cards must not use result vocabulary.
- A completed card’s spoiler-off text should omit winner, loser, score, margin, and unambiguous outcome verbs. Its spoiler-on counterpart should add the authoritative outcome.
- Conditional NBA fixtures need an explicit `notPlayed` / exemption path or equivalent metadata; treating them as ordinary completed games makes the audit misleading.
- Reuse first-party card URLs where they already exist. The one notable source-quality exception is Wallabies–France, which should remain flagged until a direct result article is recorded.
- This research does not support changing any future result: it supports status normalisation and compact preview copy only.
